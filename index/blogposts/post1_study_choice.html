My Study Choice

I want to progress into the IT world and develop my skills. I’m looking forward to seeing firsthand what my parents have glorified and why they recommended this environment. They have never lied to me and my expectations are very high. I understand that the IT world is not forgiving and I need to work hard to keep up. I have always enjoyed logical thinking and problem-solving which is why I chose this future for myself in the first place. ICT opens so many career opportunities, even though I have not chosen anything specific yet. ICT can not only change my life but also the ones around me. Growing up in my family I saw that this kind of job can support it financially and teach the new generation of a great work ethic and time management. I tend to feel the happiest when I ‘know my way around’ but also have something new to learn every day. I hope my future career meets both these conditions by learning the basics now and developing in the future.

My Motivation

Thinking about my future I hope to achieve a career that is fulfilling and suits my lifestyle. With my growing love for ICT, I have come to realize that IT IS POSSIBLE. I have always been able to keep track of everything I start doing and eventually finish it. When I’m not motivated, my biggest weapon comes to play that part – DISCIPLINE. It is human that I’m not always motivated (even though that is not the case now) but I always get the job done on time. Meanwhile, it is crucial for me to have someone to talk to when things don’t go as planned. My biggest supporters my whole life have been my family. They have held me accountable and managed to re-motivate me when I don’t feel the best. They are my biggest inspiration and the single, most important part of my life. And last but not least, im going to mention the other thing that is going to make me progress – myself. My dream is to success in life and to make that happen I need to push myself to the limit. I’m determined to keep growing in ICT and in life.
