Dream companies I want to work for:

Scuderia Ferrari

Scuderia Ferrari, founded by Enzo Ferrari in 1929 and based in Maranello, Italy, is a legendary Formula 1 team known for its iconic red cars and prancing horse logo. With a rich history of World Constructors' Championships and legendary drivers like Michael Schumacher and Niki Lauda, Ferrari is a top-tier team known for its passion and dedication to excellence.


They integrate advanced technology and IT into their operations, relying on simulations, data analytics, and collaborations with IT experts to stay competitive and innovative on the Formula 1 circuit, blending tradition with cutting-edge advancements.

Manchester City

Manchester City is a prominent football club in England's Premier League. The team plays its home matches at the Etihad Stadium, with a capacity of 53,000+. Under Pep Guardiola's leadership, the club has won multiple Premier League titles and domestic cups. The passionate fanbase, known as "Citizens," creates an electric atmosphere at their games.


Manchester City Football Club relies heavily on Information Technology (IT) for various aspects of its operations, including performance analysis, data management, fan engagement, ticketing, stadium security, financial management, scouting, and player health.
